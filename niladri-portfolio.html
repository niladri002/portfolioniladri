I'm Niladri Chatterjee, a Brand Identity & Visual Designer from India. For over four years, I've been helping businesses discover their visual voice — turning ambiguous ideas into clear, confident brands that people trust and remember.

My work spans brand identity, packaging design, social media, and web design. I believe every brand has a soul waiting to be expressed. My job is to find it, refine it, and bring it to life across every touchpoint.
